Dear App Review Team,

Thank you for your review. We respectfully appeal the Guideline 4.3(b) rejection. SBUdate is not a general-purpose dating app — it is a university-exclusive community platform designed specifically for Stony Brook University students. We believe the following features distinguish it meaningfully from existing dating apps on the App Store:

**1. University-Exclusive Access**
SBUdate is restricted to verified Stony Brook University students. Only users with an @stonybrook.edu Google account can sign in. This creates a closed, trusted community — unlike any general dating app on the App Store.

**2. Admin-Moderated Profiles**
Every new profile goes through manual admin review before becoming visible. Profiles are approved or rejected by a human moderator. This is not automated — it ensures every user is a real student with appropriate content.

**3. Like Comment Moderation**
When users send a like with a comment, that comment requires admin approval before the recipient sees it. This prevents inappropriate first messages — a problem endemic to dating apps that SBUdate actively solves.

**4. Campus-Specific Profile Fields**
Profiles include Stony Brook-specific data: actual SBU residence halls (Roosevelt, Kelly, Roth, Tabler, West Apartments, etc.), 211+ SBU majors, and graduation year. This campus-native context does not exist in any general dating app.

**5. Prompt-Based Discovery**
Users answer from 25 original conversation prompts that are interleaved with photos in a card-based layout. Discovery is driven by personality and written expression, not just photos.

**6. Turn-Based Messaging**
The matches interface organizes conversations by whose turn it is to respond — "Active" (your turn) vs "Their turn." This encourages thoughtful engagement over rapid-fire messaging.

**7. Comprehensive Safety Infrastructure**
- Community reporting system with multiple categories (harassment, impersonation, underage, spam)
- User blocking
- Profile pause (hide without deleting)
- Full account and data deletion (GDPR-compliant cascading delete of all messages, likes, matches, photos, and profile data)

**8. Designed for a Specific Community**
SBUdate serves a campus of 26,000+ students. University-specific dating fills a genuine need: students want to meet people they share a physical community with, verified by institutional credentials. This is fundamentally different from open-access dating apps that serve the general public.

- Fixed the post-login loading bug

We hope this context demonstrates that SBUdate provides a unique, community-specific experience. We would be happy to provide any additional information.
